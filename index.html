About the Game

Watch Full Trailer on YouTube

Protect Pine Valley is an innovative hybrid strategy-action game that puts you in command of defending the peaceful town of Pine Valley from relentless enemy forces. Experience the perfect blend of tactical depth and visceral combat as you seamlessly switch between two distinct gameplay modes.

In Commander Mode, take a bird's-eye view of the battlefield and orchestrate your defense strategy. Deploy troops, manage resources, and position your forces to counter incoming threats. Every decision matters as you coordinate multiple units across dynamic battlefields.

When the action heats up, instantly switch to Soldier Mode and dive into the thick of battle. Take direct control of any soldier in your army and fight enemies with precision aim and fast-paced combat. Feel the intensity of frontline warfare while your AI-controlled units continue executing your strategic commands.

Will you lead Pine Valley to victory through careful planning, decisive action, or a combination of both? The choice is yours in this unique gaming experience that delivers both strategic depth and action-packed thrills.

The Story

Pine Valley was once a peaceful town nestled in the heart of a lush forest. For generations, its inhabitants lived in harmony with nature, their homes protected by ancient pine trees that stood as silent guardians over the valley.

But peace never lasts forever. When hostile forces emerged from beyond the valley's borders, threatening to destroy everything the people of Pine Valley held dear, a call went out for defenders. As the newly appointed commander, you must rally your troops and protect the town from wave after wave of increasingly dangerous enemies.

The fate of Pine Valley rests in your hands. Will you lead from the command center, or fight alongside your soldiers on the front lines? The choice is yours.

Gameplay Features

Dual Gameplay Modes

Experience the unique freedom of switching between strategic overview and ground-level action at any moment. Plan your defense from above, then instantly jump into the action to turn the tide of battle personally.

Strategic Depth

In Commander Mode, you'll need to master resource management, troop deployment, and tactical positioning. Place defensive structures, coordinate unit movements, and anticipate enemy attack patterns to create an impenetrable defense.

Intense Action

Switch to Soldier Mode to experience fast-paced combat from a third-person perspective. Take control of any unit in your army and fight with skill-based shooting mechanics. Your actions on the battlefield can change the course of the entire battle.

Progressive Challenge

Face increasingly difficult waves of enemies, each with unique abilities and tactics. Adapt your strategy and refine your combat skills to overcome each new threat to Pine Valley.

Development Vision

Protect Pine Valley was born from a desire to merge two beloved gaming genres: real-time strategy and third-person action. We believe that players shouldn't have to choose between being a strategic mastermind or an action hero—they should be able to be both.

Built in Unity and developed by a passionate indie team in Boston, our goal is to create an experience that respects both strategic thinking and player skill, offering a game that's as rewarding to plan as it is to play.
