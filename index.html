About the Game

Protect Pine Valley is a strategy-action game where you defend the town of Pine Valley from enemy forces. The game allows you to switch between two gameplay modes.

In Commander Mode, view the battlefield from above and manage your defense strategy. Deploy troops, manage resources, and position your forces to counter incoming threats.

Switch to Soldier Mode to take direct control of any soldier in your army and fight enemies in third-person combat while your AI-controlled units continue executing your strategic commands.

Lead Pine Valley through strategic planning and direct combat action.

The Story

Pine Valley is a town in a forested valley. The inhabitants have lived there for generations.

When hostile forces attack from beyond the valley's borders, you are appointed as commander. Rally your troops and protect the town from waves of enemies.

Lead from the command center or fight alongside your soldiers on the front lines.

Gameplay Features

Dual Gameplay Modes

Switch between strategic overview and ground-level action. Plan your defense from above or control soldiers directly.

Strategy

In Commander Mode, manage resources, deploy troops, and position units. Place defensive structures and coordinate unit movements.

Action

Switch to Soldier Mode for third-person combat. Take control of any unit in your army with direct shooting mechanics.

Challenge

Face waves of enemies with different abilities and tactics.

Development Vision

Protect Pine Valley combines real-time strategy and third-person action gameplay.

Built in Unity and developed in Boston, the game focuses on both strategic thinking and player skill.
